# Voicenotes Capture Taxonomy Map

Image file: `voicenotes-capture-taxonomy-map.png`

This image is a product inspiration reference centered on the word
"Voicenotes." It presents a light, sparse concept map where captured voice notes
branch into high-level categories: Ideas, Reminders, Memories, Inspiration, and
Reflections. Each category includes example subtypes, such as startup ideas,
gift ideas, side projects, business ideas, follow ups, packing lists,
recommendations, travel moments, family stories, milestones, creative sparks,
writing prompts, book takeaways, podcast gems, personal wins, life lessons,
daily gratitude, and mood or self check-ins.

The reference is useful for thinking about Troje's information architecture and
post-capture classification. It suggests that voice capture can be framed as a
central input stream that later organizes itself into practical categories,
personal memory, reflection, inspiration, and actionable reminders.
